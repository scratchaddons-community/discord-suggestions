The ability to favorite certain addons to put in its own category. This would be useful for addons where you want to change a certain value on the fly, such as the 60 fps addon (sometimes I need to quickly change the project's fps).
To favorite an addon, you would expand it and click the star that would appear. This would be placed next to the reset addon button.
Perhaps another feature in this is the ability to drag the addons in this section around in a customized order rather than being in alphabetical order. Dunno if there would be a visual cue for this or just the user dragging the addon cards around
I'm away "Recently used" exists, but this would be a lot simpler for the user, especially since they can change things in the addon more quickly rather than scrolling through the list of addons or searching for the addon.
