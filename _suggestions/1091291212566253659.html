Things like:
Drop shadow - A simple shadow cast by the shape of the block, can be continuous (Sort of stretching the shadow) or not. The blur can also be set, and so can the transparency. The distance could also be set, by making the shadow appear at a further or closer distance to the block or script, and with distance comes angle. Angle would be the direction of the shadow.
Glow - A simple glow effect, the options for this one could be opacity and colour. Colour could have an extra option to make it the block's colour.
Bevel - As seen in 2.0 and prior.
3D effect - an extended part of the block on top to add a sort of 3D effect.
Stroke - A simple border around the block, who's colour can be altered.
Interior shading - This one I came up with myself. The "Mouths" of the If {}, if{} else {}, repeat () times {}, repeat until <> {} and forever {} blocks have a sort of backdrop (Look at the image for what it could look like)
Random shading - Making the block's colour darker or lighter randomly. This applies to all blocks, and the only setting would be the randomness value, which determines how light or dark it should be. Think of it like the Set effect [brightness] block with a pick random from (1) to (randomness) value.
That's all for now! If you'd like to add onto this post, ask questions, or provide feedback, then feel free to do so!
